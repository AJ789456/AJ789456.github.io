Steel Bridge

Steel Bridge was the first club I joined in college. In my first year on the team, I gained a lot of experience in metal fabrication, and I learned about the structure of the competition and how it affected the bridge's design. Although we didn't win any of the major awards, I had a lot of fun in my first year on the team. Now, in my second year on the team, I get to do a lot more teaching and supervision, which is another new experience for me. I make sure I continue learning about design and fabrication, but being able teach new members is fun. I'm looking forward to this year's competition because our pace this year seems great.
