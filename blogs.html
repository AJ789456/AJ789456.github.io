May 1

On May 2 the Chegg stock (CHGG) tanked; it dropped by almost 50%. This was due to the recent release of the AI chatbot ChatGPT and the resulting increase in student usage of this novel technology. Although Chegg announced that it would team up with OpenAI, the company behind ChatGPT, to create their own AI chatbot several months prior to their drop in stock price, they provided an update saying that their chatbot would not be functional for another 6 months, which was the main cause of the drop in stock price.
